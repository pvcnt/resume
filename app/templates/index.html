Vincent Primault vincent [dot] primault [at] insa-lyon.fr

I am a PhD student (October 2013 – now) at INSA Lyon (France), working in the LIRIS lab in the DRIM research team. My current research interests are privacy preservation, location privacy, spatio-temporal databases and distributed systems.

Education

  • 2013: Diploma of engineer in computer science from INSA Lyon, giving the Master's Degree, with honors.
  • 2008: Baccalauréat S, with highest honors.
  • Languages: French (native), English (TOEIC 940), German (basic skills, european level B1).

Experience

  • September 2016 – now: Lecturer at the University of Lyon 1 for 1st and 2nd year students.
  • October 2013 – now: PhD student in the LIRIS lab working on privacy-preserving access to location-based services and privacy-preserving mobility data publication.
  • December 2012 – June 2013: Internship at the LIRIS lab, working on the KISS project. Conception and development of a prototype of an application whose goal is to model data coming for various and heterogeneous data sources, index it and handle a custom query language (Java).
  • May – August 2012: Internship at Fiducial, working on the company main portal. Specification, conception and development of a interface to manage client accounts and activity related to them (eZ Publish, PHP).
  • July – August 2011: Internship at OpenClassrooms, a leading french MOOC platform. Preparation, specification, conception and development of a prototype of what should become the future version of the website (Symfony2, PHP).
